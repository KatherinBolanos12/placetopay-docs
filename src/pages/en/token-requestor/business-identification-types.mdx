# Business Identification Types

Tipo de identificación       | Descripción del valor                                      | Formato                                                                                                             | Ejemplo
-----------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------|
ABN                          | Australian business number                                 | 11 numeric digits                                                                                                   | 12345678901
BID                          | Business identification number; all countries.             | 8 numeric digits                                                                                                    | 12345678
BN                           | Canadian business number                                   | 9 numeric digits                                                                                                    | 123456789
BRNO                         | Malaysia only                                              | Up to 10 numeric digits followed by a hyphen and a single character or 12 numeric digits                            | 123456789012-1
CL                           | Qatar Commercial License Number                            | 4–6 numeric digits                                                                                                  | 12345
CLN                          | Kuwait Company License Number                              | 5–7 numeric digits                                                                                                  | 1234567
CNPJ                         | Brazil only                                                | 14 alphanumeric characters, identified by n in the example, and punctuation characters (period, slash, and hyphen). | nn.nnn.nnn/nnnn-nn
CR                           | Saudi Arabia Company Registration Number                   | 10–12 numeric digits                                                                                                | 1234567890
CUIT                         | Ukraine Ukrainian individual entrepeneurs                  | 10 numeric digits                                                                                                   | 1234567890
EIN                          | US Federal tax ID (US only)                                | 9 numeric digits                                                                                                    | 123456789
HKBR                         | Hong Kong only                                             | 11 numeric digits and a hyphen                                                                                      | 12345678-123
NATIONAL_ID                  | Dominican Republic Cedula de Identidad (Merchant Owner ID) | DO + 11 digits                                                                                                      | DO01234567890
NZBN                         | New Zealand business number                                | 13 numeric digits                                                                                                   | 1234567890123
PAN                          | Indian business identification number                      | 10 alphanumeric characters                                                                                          | abc1234567
RFC                          | Mexico only                                                | 12 alphanumeric characters for corporations; 13 alphanumeric characters for individuals.                            | abc1234567890
RNC                          | Dominican Republic (National Contributors Number)          | DO + 9 digits                                                                                                       | DO123456789
RUC                          | Peru only                                                  | 11 numeric digits                                                                                                   | 12345678901
RUT                          | Chile and Columbia only                                    | 9 numeric digits; for Chile; 10 numeric digits for Columbia                                                         | 1234567890
SIN                          | Canadian social insurance number                           | 9 numeric digits                                                                                                    | 123456789
SSN                          | US social security number (US only)                        | 9 numeric digits                                                                                                    | 123456789
UEN                          | Singapore only                                             | 9 or 10 alphanumeric characters                                                                                     | 123456789
