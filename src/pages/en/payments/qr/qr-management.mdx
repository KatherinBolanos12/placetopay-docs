## Share the QR image

Clicking on the QR option will display the corresponding image, which can be saved, copied, scanned and shared by any available means (email or social network).  
 In addition, this image can be printed to have it physically visible, however, before printing it is important to keep in mind that when enlarging the image to adjust the print size the dimensions (width and height) 
 must be the same size on each side to avoid distortions in the pixels.
