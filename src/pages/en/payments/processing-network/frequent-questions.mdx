# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Transaction charged but declined in Placetopay panel
If your merchant's transaction appears declined in the Placetopay administrative panel, but you have the support that the money was debited to the user or you see the credit in your accounts, you must send a request to the processing network to which the franchise of the card used in the transaction belongs in order to validate the real status of the transaction. Once you obtain the confirmation from the entity, you must send a request to servicioposventa@placetopay.com with the corresponding supports to update the status of the transaction.

## Transaction approved and uncompensated 

### Gateway:

In this case, you must perform the above mentioned process and make the query before the processing network as indicated, later the answer provided by the network you must share it with your collecting bank, since the clearing process is treated between processing network - collecting bank.

### Aggregator:
In case you are integrated under the aggregator model, please contact our mailbox at servicioposventa@placetopay.com.
