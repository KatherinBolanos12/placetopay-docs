## Restrictions
- **Expiration Date**: If you set the value of the `CreateSessionRequest.expiration` field, it must be at least 30 minutes after the moment the session is created.
- **Remaining Session Time**: When selecting cash payment, the remaining session time must be at least 30 minutes in order to process the payment.
