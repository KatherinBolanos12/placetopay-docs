## Payment view  {{ id: 'payments', tag: '', label: '' }}
Upon opening the payment link, whether via email, QR code, direct URL or WhatsApp message, the following information will be displayed: the expiration date, the value to be paid, the merchant to which the payment will be made, the description of the payment, the reference and the status of the payment link.{{ className: 'lead' }}
